Physics Playground

Mouse:
  • Left-click + drag: rotate camera (OrbitControls)
  • Scroll: zoom
  • Right-click + drag: pan camera
  • Middle-click / Ctrl+Left: drag physics body
Objects:
  • Stackable boxes & spheres
  • Simple hinge door
  • Basic ragdoll
Slow motion scales physics time-step (good for observing joints).
